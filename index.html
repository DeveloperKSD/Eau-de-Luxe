Frequently Asked Questions

Niche perfumes are unique, high-quality fragrances crafted by artisan perfumers or smaller, exclusive perfume houses. Unlike mass-produced perfumes, niche perfumes focus on rare ingredients, distinct scents, and exceptional craftsmanship, making them unique and personal.

Designer perfumes are created by popular fashion brands or well-known designers and are often widely available. They’re typically crafted for broader appeal, with a balance of quality and affordability. Niche perfumes, however, are produced in smaller batches, focus on unique, artistic scents, and are often harder to find.
